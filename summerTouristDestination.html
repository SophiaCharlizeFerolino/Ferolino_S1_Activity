Discover Kangsanto Spring: Your Budget-Friendly Summer Getaway!

Banhigan, Badian, Cebu, Philippines

Are you longing for a peaceful getaway that won't cost you a fortune? Well, you're in luck! Kangsanto Spring is like a hidden gem tucked away in the lush, tropical beauty of Banhigan, Badian, Cebu. This amazing cold spring is the perfect spot for tourists who want to unwind and soak up nature's beauty without spending too much money. It's a budget-friendly paradise waiting to be discovered!

With just a price of 100 pesos you can already enjoy its famed cold waters. Just visit them during their operating hours from 8:00am to 5:00pm.

Kangsanto Spring is indeed a dream come true for budget-conscious travelers. But here's the twist – they have just a handful of rooms to offer. Don't let this opportunity slip through your fingers! Take charge of your adventure and secure your reservation right away. You can pay 500 pesos reservation fee to guarantee your spot in this hidden gem. You have two options:

  • Gcash: 0917 650 4211 (Globe)
  • Bank Transfer to BDO or PNB (Please contact for details)

Before you embark on your Kangsanto Spring adventure, keep these essential tips in mind. Bring your own food to enjoy during your visit. However, please note that there's a corkage fee of 200 pesos for each type of soft drink or liquor you bring in. But fret not! Kangsanto Spring has you covered with an in-house bar that can cater to your beverage needs and keep you refreshed throughout your stay. If you've brought your own barbecue and fancy grilling it on-site, Kangsanto Spring also offers the convenience of using their grill facilities.

Finally, please note that there is a parking fee in place: 20 pesos for motorbikes, 30 pesos for tricycles, and 50 pesos for cars.

Now that you've got the details on everything you need, it's time to pack your bags and journey to Kangsanto Spring, where you can be sure to savor the cool waters that await you.
